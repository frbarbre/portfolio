about me

My name is Frederik Barbré, i’m 22 years old, and are currently studying multimedia design at Business Academy Aarhus.

I grew up in a little town just outside of Silkeborg, and i’ve known from a quite a young age that i want to work with design. My first project was at the age of 15, where i drew the intire Game of Thrones map in Adobe Illustrator.

Since then, have i dwelled a bit into Game design, by going to Vallekilde Højskole, and completing 2 semesters of Mediology at AAU.
Even though i love game design, i found myself more compelled towards UI, UX and productdesign, which has resulted in me studying to be a multimediadesigner.

analogue - Website

This is my first solo project at multimedia design.

We were to make an online magazine that included 3 written articles.

I decided to make my magazine about vinyls, because it’s quite a big hobby of mine, and i found visually atracting.

SPILGUIDEN - Website

My very first web project, the website is made purely with HTML & CSS.

The idea with SPILGUIDEN was to make a webpage where we can store information about board games, to help various board game cafés with inspirering their costumers to try new board games, through our website.

development time: 1 week

WeEat - Mobile Application

This is my 2. semester project at Mediology on AAU, the focus of the project was to make an application that could help lowering food waste.

WeEat focuses on social dining, and strives to make it easier for people to organize dinner groups with either their friends, or people they haven’t met yet.

development time: 4 months

WinterLand - Videogame

Winterland was a solo project for the exam of a course called Introduction to creative digital development at AAU.

The game should include at least 5 2D & 3D sprites, and should be coded in c# using the Unity engine.

I’m quite proud of the game as it was my first solo project.

development time: 2 weeks
